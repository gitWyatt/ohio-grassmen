About Us:

The rise of the new recreational cannabis market in Ohio has created a vacuum of information needed for the consumers. Ohioans have a tough cannabis economy to work with already and when it comes to spending money on product, it can be tough to make an educated decision. One look at a jar and a person can get information overload. That’s when we, the Ohio Grassmen, were needed.

We are the Ohio Grassmen Podcast, a local group of Northern Ohioans that love to partake in cannabis. Our mission is to help educate our viewers on the products that are offered to them in Ohio. We will do this through reviewing product, enlightening the science behind cannabis, and researching the laws that are springing up across the Buckeye State. Through doing this, we will help our community and therefore cultivate a successful cannabis economy for Ohio. For a well educated community can maintain a healthy and safe practice of the great grass that is cannabis.

Our Vision is to become a pillar of improvement for the cannabis community as a whole. We would like to have educational programming provided to as many as possible. Ohio Grassmen being a household name for knowing your strains, ratings, and terp tips is our inevitable future. With this will come a state of the best informed buyer’s market, and therefore healthiest cannabis economy possible. Also we want a strain named after us.
